Jagbir Rathee

Dr.Jagbir Rathee is an Creative Artist of folk music , theater and Literature. He was born in Jind and is currently staying in Rohtak (Haryana).

Writer poet singer actor and theatre artist Well known stand up comedy performer with haryanvi flavour Singer and writer of golden global song BOL TERE MITHE MITHE. Bollywood actor with MOVIE DANGAL with Amir Khan Performed in various countries like USA UK OMAN THAILAND PAKISTAN.

ACHIEVEMENTS
Approximately 300 awards in various cultural events like Music, Dance, Drama, Literary, Fine Arts etc. at College, State and National level. Released 35 Haryanvi entertaining albums with comedy and folk music contents. Writer and director in various T.V. serials at DD National, G.TV, DD Metro, DD Hisar.
