Mahendra Ajanabi

If you have to name the gentle string between the satire and humour, then it should be Mahendra Ajnabi. Over a period of last three decades, the history of Hindi poets-conglemerations have became great fans of this unique poet’s poetry. Mahendra Ajnabi is basically from Uttar Pradesh region. For the last three decades, Ajnabi became synonym for humorous poetry by bringing the common issues of the life to the public forum through the humour medium. Kaka Hathrasi Award, Omprakash Aditya Award are among those many awards with which Mahendra’s Hindi poetic efficiencies were honoured. He always live with his simplicity and this simple living is the style which makes him unique.

Birth : 24 June 1959
Education : M.Sc. (Botany); B.Ed.

Published work
1) Hansa-Hansa Ke Marunga (Poetry)
2) Articles, poems and satires are regularly publishing with various news papers and magazines i.e. Saptahik Hindustan, Madhuri, Parag, Dainik Hindustan, Navbharat Times, Navbharat, Sandhya Times, Rajasthan Patrika, Amar Ujala, Uttar Ujala, Bijnor Times etc.

Other achievements
1) Broadcasted with various media channels i.e. AIR, Doordarshan, Sony TV, Zee TV, Zee India, NEPC, Jain TV, SAB TV, NDTV, Aaj Tak, Tez, Delhi Aaj Tak, SAB TV, India TV, Live India, Jansandesh, Dabang, Dhamaal etc.
2) Recited poem more than 3500 kavi-sammelans all over the world i.e.USA, Thailand, Hongkong, Indonasia, Oman, UAE, Nepal etc.

Awards-
1) Awarded by President of India Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma in the year 1996.
2) Om Prakash Aditya Samman in the year 2001.
3) Awarded by Prime Minister of India Mr. Atal Bihari Vajpayee in the year 2001.
4) Kaka Hathrasi Hasya Ratna in the year 2003.
5) Kaka Hathrasi of Hindi Sahitya academy by Chief minister of Delhi in the year 2005.

Current Location : New Delhi
