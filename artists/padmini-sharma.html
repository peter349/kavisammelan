Padmini Sharma

Padmini Sharma, born on 13 December in a renowned literary family. She is one of the leading poetess of Hindi Kavi Sammelan. She is the wonderful poetess of emotion and compassion. She represented various shade of emotion of women through her poetry. She achieved lots of fame in a very young age because of her deeply sensitive poetry. Padmini Sharma received lots of affection for her geet “maine bharat ki naari hu” and “mora samariya na samjhe mere maan ki peer”. She is very-very melodious poetess and she capable in raising the wave of love and compassion in the heart of her audience. She has done number of her poetic performance live on stage of Kavi Sammelan and TV. She received lots of appreciation from all corner of the world for her magnificent and melodious poetic performance.
