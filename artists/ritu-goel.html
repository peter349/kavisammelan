Kavi Parichay

Ritu Goel

Ritu Goel is a poetess of emotions and relations. While recitation of her poetry she puts a drop of memories in the eyes of audience. She often sings while her performance, but she talks in her poetry and let people bound to get emotional with a smile on their lips. She draw her words on the canvas of family values and cultural treasure.

Education : Master of Journalism & Mass Communication.
Occupation : Direction & Production of Documentaries.

Published work
1) ANANYA (Poetry)
2) Yahin Ho Aas-Paas (Poetry)
3) Articles, poems, interviews and stories are regularly publishing with various news papers and magazines.

Other achievements
1) Municipal Corporator from BJP elected in April 2017.
1) Broadcasted with various media i.e. Doordarshan, All India radio, NDTV, Life OK, SAB TV, Jain TV, Jansandesh, Dabang etc.
2) Recited poem more then 1000 kavi-sammelans all over the country.
3) Founder president of reputed NGO Disha Foundation.
4) Running her own advertising agency and event management company named ‘Sanskriti Creations’
5) Actively associated with various literary & social NGO’s.
6) Produced and directed various documentaries for Ministry of agriculture, Ministry of Culture, HRD, Ministry of health, Sahitya Acadamy, Lalit Kala Acadamy, IGNOU, NIOS and various corporate houses.
7) Visited overseas for kavi-sammelans i.e. Unighted Kingdom with the sponcership of ICCR.

Awards-
1) National award for social work 2003 by Nehru Yuva Kendra.
2) Awarded by Hindi Academy, Delhi.
