Sampat Saral

Sampat Saral is a Hindi poet and satirist. He was born on April 8, 1962 at Shekhawati in Rajasthan. After studying from village Shakhawati and Jaipur, he has completed his Master of Arts (Hindi) and B. Ed. from Rajasthan University.

He writes in Hindi and Rajasthani languages and has published Chaaki Dekh Chunav Ki (Look at the pivot of Elections), collection of Rajasthani poetry. Saral has also recorded few audio and video albums under Veena cassette, and has penned lyrics for various Hindi TV serials.

With his satirical poetry, Saral he has toured USA, Canada, Oman, Singapore, Hong Kong, UAE, Thailand and Nepal. He is a poet of the same stream as Sharad Joshi, Surendra Sharma and K. P. Saxena. He performs in Kavi sammelan across India and world. He is among very few performers who read blank-verse in kavi-sammelan.
